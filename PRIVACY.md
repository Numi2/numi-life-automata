# Numi Automata Privacy Policy

Effective July 23, 2026

Numi Automata does not collect, store, transmit, sell, or share personal data.
The app has no accounts, analytics, advertising, tracking, external network
service, or in-app purchases. Simulation state is processed locally on the
user's Mac.

For privacy questions, contact [numan@thabit.no](mailto:numan@thabit.no).
